The Problem

"More than 3.5 million people in this country never leave their homes. Of these, 1.9 million (54 percent) are people with disabilities."

United States Department of Transportation

What is Sherpa?

Sherpa is an accessibility focused touring application that encourages users to explore their local area, while also meeting others similar to themselves.

The Value

We aim to change the mentality of wheelchair users through accessible self-guided tours that allow users to explore their local area.

Key Features

Solo Tour

Explore your local area alone with our solo accessibility tour feature.

Group Tour

Join a tour group, or make a tour group to go together on a group tour.

Gamified Profile

With every tour gain miles to level up!
