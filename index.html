SHERPA

Opening your world, one tour at a time.

"More than 3.5 million people in this country never leave their homes. Of these, 1.9 million (54 percent) are people with disabilities."

United States Department of Transportation

Why might this be?

  • Airlines commonly damage expensive wheelchairs, making travel a struggle.

What we found:

Wheelchair users are often convinced by social constructions, and lack of inspiration that travel is only for the able-bodied.

With SHERPA we aim to bring wheelchair users one step closer to travel, by first allowing them to explore their local communities.
